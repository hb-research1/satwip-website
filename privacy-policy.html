Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Hudson Barry Research ("we," "us," or "our"), developer of SATWIP (the "App"), handles information in connection with your use of the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

SATWIP collects a minimal amount of information, limited to one specific event described in section 2 below. In all other respects:

  • We do not require you to register or create an online account with us.
  • We do not use tracking technologies such as cookies, analytics pixels, or telemetry beacons within the App.
  • All screening criteria, saved results, and preferences are stored locally on your own device and are never transmitted to us.

2. Disclaimer Acceptance Log

The App displays a research disclaimer that must be acknowledged before the App can be used. The first time each account accepts the disclaimer (and again if the disclaimer is updated), the following information is transmitted to and stored in our private database (hosted by Supabase on AWS):

  • Your name and email address (as entered during App setup)
  • The date and time of acceptance
  • The App version and disclaimer version number

This record is retained for compliance purposes and is not shared with any third party. The database is configured so that stored records cannot be read, modified, or deleted by any external party — only the developer has access. Data is transmitted over HTTPS.

3. Data the App Fetches from Third Parties

SATWIP queries the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission's publicly available EDGAR API to retrieve financial filing data. As required by the SEC's fair-use policy, SATWIP sends the name and email address you configure as your SEC API Identity as a User-Agent header with each EDGAR request. Your SEC API Identity may be the same as your account name and email or different — you configure it separately in the App. No other personal information is transmitted to EDGAR.

SATWIP also queries third-party market data providers (including Yahoo Finance and Nasdaq) to retrieve share price and market capitalisation data. These requests include only ticker symbols — publicly-traded security identifiers that do not constitute personal information — and do not include your name or email address.

The following table summarises what is sent to each third party and why:

RecipientWhat is sentWhy
SEC EDGAR Your SEC API Identity name and email (as a User-Agent header); company CIK numbers Required by the SEC's fair-use policy for API access
Yahoo Finance Ticker symbols To fetch share prices and market capitalisation
Nasdaq Ticker symbols To fetch market capitalisation as a fallback
Supabase (hosted on AWS) Name, email, timestamp, App and disclaimer version Disclaimer acceptance log (section 2)

The SEC's own privacy and data policies govern the EDGAR API. You can review those at www.sec.gov/privacy.

4. Information Stored on Your Device

All other data created or saved by SATWIP is stored locally on your device only, in the folder C:\Users\<you>\.satwip\. This includes:

  • Your name, email address, and account name
  • Notes, BUY/HOLD/SELL signals, and comments you write for individual companies
  • Custom filter sets you save for reuse
  • Tickers you have marked as dropped

Except as described above, none of this information is uploaded, synced, or transmitted to any party.

5. Information Not Collected

SATWIP does not collect or transmit:

  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data
  • Financial account numbers or brokerage credentials
  • Payment information

6. Microsoft Store

SATWIP is available on the Microsoft Store. Microsoft may collect diagnostic, usage, and purchase information as part of their platform. This data is collected by Microsoft, not by us, and is governed by Microsoft's own privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

SATWIP is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
