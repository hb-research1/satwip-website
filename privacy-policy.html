Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Hudson Barry Research ("we," "us," or "our"), developer of SATWIP (the "App"), handles information in connection with your use of the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

SATWIP collects a limited amount of personal information, as described in the sections below. In all other respects:

  • We do not use tracking technologies such as cookies, analytics pixels, or telemetry beacons within the App.
  • All screening criteria, saved results, and preferences are stored locally on your own device and are never transmitted to us.

2. Email Verification

Before using the App, you are required to verify your email address using a one-time code sent to that address. If you configure a different email address for your SEC API Identity, that address must also be verified separately.

During email verification, your email address is transmitted to Supabase, Inc. (our authentication service provider, hosted on AWS), which creates a persistent user record containing your email address and a timestamp. Supabase uses Resend, Inc. (an email delivery service) to send the verification code to your address; Resend receives your email address solely for that purpose. A session credential is stored locally on your device after successful verification so the App can re-authenticate you automatically at startup.

3. Disclaimer Acceptance Log

The App displays a disclaimer that must be acknowledged before the App can be used. The first time each account accepts the disclaimer (and again if the disclaimer is updated), the following information is transmitted to and stored in our private database (hosted by Supabase on AWS):

  • Your name and email address (as entered during App setup)
  • The date and time of acceptance
  • The App version and disclaimer version number

This record is retained for compliance purposes and is not shared with any third party. The database is configured so that stored records cannot be read, modified, or deleted by any external party — only the developer has access. Data is transmitted over HTTPS.

The following table summarises the third-party services that receive personal information from the App, and why. The name and email address you configure as your SEC API Identity are sent as a User-Agent header with each EDGAR request, as required by the SEC's fair-use policy; your SEC API Identity may be the same as your account name and email or different — you may configure it separately in the App.

RecipientWhat is sentWhy
SEC EDGAR Your SEC API Identity name and email (as a User-Agent header); company CIK numbers Required by the SEC's fair-use policy for API access
Supabase Email address Email verification (section 2); stores a user record in our authentication service

Hudson Barry Research Name, email, timestamp, App and disclaimer version Disclaimer acceptance log (section 3)

SATWIP also queries Yahoo Finance and Nasdaq to retrieve share price and market capitalisation data. These requests include only ticker symbols and no personal information.

In addition, your device's IP address is inherently transmitted to every service the App connects to as a technical requirement of internet communication. This is a standard property of the TCP/IP protocol and is not specific to SATWIP.

The SEC's own privacy and data policies govern the EDGAR API. You can review those at www.sec.gov/privacy.

5. Information Stored on Your Device

All other data created or saved by SATWIP is stored locally on your device only:

  • Windows: C:\Users\<you>\.satwip\
  • macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/SATWIP/

Data stored here includes:

  • Your account name and email address
  • Your profile name(s)
  • Your SEC API Identity name and email (stored per profile; may differ from your account credentials)
  • Notes, BUY/HOLD/SELL signals, and comments you write for individual companies
  • Custom filter sets you save for reuse
  • Tickers you have marked as dropped

Except as described above, none of this information is uploaded, synced, or transmitted to any party.

6. Information Not Collected

SATWIP does not collect or transmit:

  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data
  • Financial account numbers or brokerage credentials
  • Payment information

7. App Stores

SATWIP is available on the Microsoft Store and is planned to be released on the Apple App Store. Microsoft and Apple may each collect diagnostic, usage, and purchase information as part of their respective platforms. This data is collected by Microsoft or Apple, not by us, and is governed by their own privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

SATWIP is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
