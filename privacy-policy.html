Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SATWIP ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the SATWIP Windows desktop application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

SATWIP does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information about you. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any other identifying information.
  • We do not use tracking technologies such as cookies, analytics pixels, or telemetry beacons within the App.
  • All data you enter into the App (screening criteria, saved results, preferences) is stored locally on your own device and is never transmitted to us.

2. Data the App Fetches from Third Parties

SATWIP queries the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission's publicly available EDGAR API to retrieve financial filing data. This is a one-way, read-only request to a public government database. Your identity is not transmitted to EDGAR as part of these requests; only the specific financial data query (e.g., a company ticker or CIK number) is sent as part of the API call.

The SEC's own privacy and data policies govern the EDGAR API. You can review those at www.sec.gov/privacy.

3. Microsoft Store

SATWIP is distributed through the Microsoft Store. Microsoft may collect diagnostic, usage, and purchase information as part of their platform in accordance with their own privacy policies. This data is collected by Microsoft, not by us. Please review Microsoft's Privacy Statement for details.

4. Children's Privacy

SATWIP is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because we collect no personal information from any user, no special measures are required beyond this notice.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact

For information about data accuracy, calculation methodology, and our disclaimer regarding financial advice, see the Disclaimer on the SATWIP home page.
