Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Hudson Barry Research ("we," "us," or "our"), developer of SATWIP – Stock Analysis Tool (the "App"), handles information in connection with your use of the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

SATWIP collects a minimal amount of information, limited to one specific event described in section 2 below. In all other respects:

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not use tracking technologies such as cookies, analytics pixels, or telemetry beacons within the App.
  • All screening criteria, saved results, and preferences are stored locally on your own device and are never transmitted to us.

2. Disclaimer Acceptance Log

On first use, the App displays a research disclaimer that must be acknowledged before the App can be used. When you click "I Understand," the following information is transmitted to and stored in our private database (hosted by Supabase on AWS):

  • Your name and email address (as entered during App setup)
  • The date and time of acceptance
  • The App version and disclaimer version number

This record is retained for compliance purposes and is not shared with any third party. It is accessible only to the developer. The database is configured so that this information cannot be read, modified, or deleted by any external party — only the developer has read access. Data is transmitted over HTTPS.

3. Data the App Fetches from Third Parties

SATWIP queries the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission's publicly available EDGAR API to retrieve financial filing data. This is a one-way, read-only request to a public government database. Your identity is not transmitted to EDGAR as part of these requests; only the specific financial data query (e.g., a company ticker or CIK number) is sent as part of the API call.

SATWIP also queries third-party market data providers (including Yahoo Finance and Nasdaq) to retrieve share price and market capitalisation data. These requests do not include your name or email address.

The SEC's own privacy and data policies govern the EDGAR API. You can review those at www.sec.gov/privacy.

4. Microsoft Store

SATWIP is distributed through the Microsoft Store. Microsoft may collect diagnostic, usage, and purchase information as part of their platform in accordance with their own privacy policies. This data is collected by Microsoft, not by us. Please review Microsoft's Privacy Statement for details.

5. Children's Privacy

SATWIP is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

For information about data accuracy, calculation methodology, and our disclaimer regarding financial advice, see the Disclaimer on the SATWIP home page.
