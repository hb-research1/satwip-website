What SATWIP Does

📊

Screen with Real Filing Data

Pull reported figures directly from SEC EDGAR filings — 10-Ks and 10-Qs — then screen thousands of public companies using ratios calculated locally from those figures.

🔍

Customizable Criteria

Set your own thresholds for revenue, earnings, debt, and more. SATWIP surfaces the companies that match your specific investment strategy.

🖥️

Native Windows App

Built as a native Windows desktop application — fast, offline-capable for data you've already fetched, and no subscription required.

💻

Local Compute

Screening runs entirely on your machine. Data is fetched directly from its source — no subscription aggregator in the middle, no cloud service logging your queries. What you screen stays yours.

🏛️

Transparent, Authoritative Data

All fundamental financial data comes from the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission's public EDGAR database — the same source institutional investors rely on, and free to access.

About SATWIP

SATWIP was built for individual investors who want to do their own research without paying for expensive data platforms. By querying the SEC's EDGAR API directly, SATWIP gives you access to the same financial disclosures that every public company is required to file — quarterly earnings, annual reports, balance sheets, and more.

The application runs entirely on your Windows machine. Your screens, filters, and saved results stay local and private.

macOS and Linux builds are on the roadmap. In the meantime, Windows 10 and Windows 11 users can download SATWIP from the Microsoft Store today.
