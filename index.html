About SATWIP

SATWIP is being developed by an individual investor who wanted to automate the screening phase of his fundamental investment analysis without paying for expensive third party software. By querying the SEC's EDGAR API directly, SATWIP pulls financial figures from the quarterly and annual reports that public companies are required to file — revenue, profit, balance sheet items, and more — at very low cost.

The application runs entirely on your Windows machine. Your screens, filters, and saved results stay local and private.

macOS and Linux builds are on the roadmap. In the meantime, Windows 10 and Windows 11 users will soon be able to download SATWIP from the Microsoft Store.

Core functionality is and will always be free, and will continue to be refined over time. A premium tier with additional features is in development.
