About SATWIP

SATWIP was built by an individual investor who wanted to do fundamental screening without paying for expensive third-party software and without trusting a data aggregator's interpretation of company filings. By querying the SEC's EDGAR API directly, SATWIP pulls financial figures from the quarterly and annual reports that public companies file — revenue, profit, balance sheet items, and more — at very low cost. SATWIP is direct and transparent because its input figures come straight from company filings with the SEC, and Debug mode shows you exactly which filing and XBRL tag produced each figure. No middleman. No interpretation. You can verify anything.

The application runs entirely on your Windows machine. Your screens, filters, and saved results stay local and private.

SATWIP's future includes distributions for macOS and Linux. Windows 10 and Windows 11 users can download SATWIP now from the Microsoft Store.

Core functionality is free. A premium tier is in development.
